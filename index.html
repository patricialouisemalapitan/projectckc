    "feed your inner artist"

    Crafts and Snacks is determined to become a daily necessity for local artists and foodies, a place to dream of as you escape the daily stresses of life and just a comfortable place to feed yourself a fun snack or unleash your inner artist, all in one. With the growing demand for high-quality crafties, snackos and great service, Crafts and Snacks offers everybody the best prepared drinks that will be complimented with different variety of foods, as well as high-quality art materials which the customers may buy and use as they go home.
